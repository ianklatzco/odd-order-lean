## Task 1: PF-side character infrastructure (inertia, Clifford, supported calculus)

Prerequisites PF1/PF5 assume but M2 deferred. New
`OddOrder/Mathlib/RepresentationTheory/Inertia.lean` (+ additions to ClassFunction.lean's
supportedOn section where natural):
- Conjugate class functions/characters under G-conjugation of a NORMAL subgroup's class
  functions: `ClassFunction.conjg`-shaped (`(φ ^ g)(h) = φ (g⁻¹ h g)`-convention — match
  MathComp `cfConjg` via grep of PFsection1.v usage), action of G on `Irr H` for `H ⊴ G`,
  the inertia subgroup `inertia θ` (stabilizer, contains H), MathComp `'I_G[θ]`.
- Clifford at character level (MathComp `Clifford_Res_sum_cfclass`-shaped): for `H ⊴ G`,
  `θ ∈ Irr H` a constituent of `res χ`: `res χ = e • ∑ orbit of θ` (sum over the G-orbit
  `cfclass θ`); constituents of `res` lie in one orbit; `⟪res (ind θ), θ⟫`-facts. Port at the
  strength PF1's (1.5) consumes (read PFsection1.v:150-260) — not full Clifford correspondence
  (defer (1.7)'s inertia-induction bijection to Task 2 where PF1 proves it).
- Supported calculus: `'CF(G, A)'` as a submodule of ClassFunction (build on the existing
  `supportedOn`), cfInner restricted-support lemmas, the "vanishing off A" ↔ membership API,
  and `ClassFunction.ind`/`res` interaction with supports (ind of A-supported is
  class-support-of-A supported — the shapes PF2 needs; read PFsection2.v hypotheses).
- NAME_MAP rows: `cfConjg`, `cfclass`, `'I_G[θ]`, `inertia_Clifford` family, `'CF(G,A)`.
